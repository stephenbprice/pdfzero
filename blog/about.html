What

PDFZero is a free, private PDF toolkit in your browser.

Free. PDFZero is free to use. That's it - no limits on bandwith, no limits on uses-per-hour, no subscription, no freemium. Free.

Private. Your files never leave your computer. Seriously - it works offline. When you visit a tool page, all of the code to modify your PDFs is downloaded as part of the webpage. Everything happens on your own computer, so nothing needs to be uploaded to the cloud.

PDF Toolkit. PDFZero is a collection of tools to do simple operations on PDF documents. With our tools, you can:

  • Merge two or more PDFs (PDF Organizer)
  • Rotate and delete pages in a PDF (PDF Organizer)
  • Fill and flatten a PDF form (PDF Form Filler)
  • Add text to a PDF (PDF Editor)
  • Split a single PDF into multiple PDFs (PDF Splitter)
  • Mail merge a PDF form with a spreadsheet (PDF Mail Merger)
  • More coming soon

In your browser. Our webpages are our tools. Everyting you need to manipulate your documents is included in the html page itself. No need to install anything from us - just come prepared with a modern web browser.

How

Our site is built on top of some really awesome open-source tools. Notably, PDF.JS and PDF-Lib. These tools in turn allow us to display PDFs in your browser and manipulate them in pure javascript. And because javascript can run in a browser, you can use all of the tools without the files ever leaving your computer.

Why

I do a lot of work with PDFs in my day job, and I know how important it is for small and medium-sized companies or departments to have PDF tools available for document management. With the development of the awesome open-source programming libraries above, I realized that many of the PDF tools typically hidden behind a paywall can be provided free of charge. We can afford free because we have no server costs - all of the computing power you use is on your own computer.
